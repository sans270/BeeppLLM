There is a backpack that is made out of half of a cylinder placed on top of a rectangular prism. 
Write a python program that finds the volume of the backpack when given the length, width, and height of the rectangular prism. 
It is also given the units , such as square feet or square inches, that the backpack is to be measured with. 
It prints the volume of the backpack without being rounded, and also the volume of the backpack when rounded to the nearest tenth.
